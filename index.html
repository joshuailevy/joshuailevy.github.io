Joshua I. Levy


I'm a PhD Candidate in the Department of Engineering Sciences and Applied Mathematics (ESAM) at Northwestern University, coadvised by William Kath (ESAM) and Neda Bagheri (ChBE, now at University of Washington). Most of my research is on Drosophila thermosensory navigation, in close collaboration with the Gallio Lab in the Department of Neurobiology (Northwestern).

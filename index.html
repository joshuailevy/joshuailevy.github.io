Joshua Levy


I'm a graduate student in the department of Engineering Sciences and Applied Mathematics (ESAM) at Northwestern University. I am co-advised by William Kath (ESAM) and Neda Bagheri (Chemical and Biological Engineering). My research focuses on using data to accurately model dynamical systems encountered in the brain and during cancer formation. I seek to build upon realistic models to determine control strategies for these complex systems.
